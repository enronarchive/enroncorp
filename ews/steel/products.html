Helping Steel Producers, Processors, Service Centers and Consumers Improve Their Business

From fluctuating raw material costs to swings in market demand for finished steel, producers, processors and service centers can face enormous challenges in maintaining profit margins and cash flows. For consumers of steel, the volatility of prices can wreak havoc with the cost of manufacturing their finished products. Yet investors and lenders are demanding greater stability in the growth of revenues, earnings and cash flows. For solutions to this apparent paradox, turn to Enron. As a leading provider of price risk management services, we can help you buy and/or sell steel more efficiently, hedge the risks associated with steel pricing, and ultimately, lower your cost of capital. We also give you the ability to lock in the purchase/sales price of steel for multiple years. We'll review your risk exposure and suggest risk-reducing strategies for taking control of your steel price exposure.
