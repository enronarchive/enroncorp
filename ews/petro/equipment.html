Equipment finanacing

Enron integrates its significant industry experience as well as its multi-billion dollar debt and equity financing expertise to construct unique, cost-efficient and flexible financing alternatives for your expansion needs. Whether your company is public or private, Enron can work with you to design a more efficient, cost-effective capital structure or fund further growth.

For moderate-to-low risk capital equipment projects, Enron's mezzanine transactions fill the gap between equity and debt. Mezzanine financing is flexible, designed to benefit from tax and accounting advantages and to permit you to retain as much control of your projects as possible. We can structure limited partnerships, overriding royalty interests, net profit interests and working interests to satisfy your capital needs.
