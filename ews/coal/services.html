Our Services

For centuries, coal has provided a cost-effective source of heat and energy. More than 55% of electric power in the U.S. is generated by coal. The U.S. electric utility industry uses about 85% of the country's total annual coal production, or 850 million tons. Overseas, coal continues its rapid growth as a mainstay of electric power generation.
