Recognizing Coal Financing and Investment Opportunities

Competing successfully requires resources, which typically create a need for new sources of capital. At Enron, we are continually exploring alternative methods of securing affordable equity and debt capital for our customers. Since 1991, we have committed over $3.5 billion to the financing needs of the energy industry.

Enron structures transactions to maximize capitalization possibilities and manage commodity risk for coal, oil and gas producers and energy service firms, as well as downstream and end-user companies. By leveraging existing assets, restructuring debt and building equity relationships, we help customers achieve greater efficiency and profitability.

Through our finance activities, Enron will participate in financial opportunities with domestic and international coal companies with significant capital requirements, quality management and low-cost operations.

Your Creative Financing Source

While other commercial institutions may offer energy financing, our ability to combine financial and physical capabilities enables us to structure creative transactions that are optimally structured to enhance your company's position. We view ourselves not only as a source of financing, but also your ally, ready to commit growth capital (both debt and equity) and physical purchases and sales to help take your company to the next level.
