In two years, we have completed more than 150 transactions and traded over one million allowances. In short, we understand the issues that confront environmental management today. Example Your current supplies of emission allowances far exceed short-term needs. However, corporate concerns regarding possible future requirements are keeping these assets from being fully utilized. What should a manager do?
Solution 1 Sell excess allowances but retain the right to buy them back at a future date.
Result 1 Large cash inflow available for immediate investment, plus opportunity to meet future needs at a known price.
Solution 2 Exchange your current allowances for more allowances in the future.
Result 2 Let your allowances work for you now, increasing asset value for possible future sale or use.
Solution 3 Exchange your current allowances for the same number of allowances in the future plus cash now.
Result 3 Same as Result 2 above, but you gain cash value up front.
