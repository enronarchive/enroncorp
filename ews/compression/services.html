Our Services

At Enron Compression Services (ECS), we believe that a horsepower solution is much more than just an installation of new equipment. We analyze every aspect of your project and discover the optimal way to achieve your business goals. By offering a selection of various bundled and unbundled services, ECS can provide the most flexible and cost-effective solution to meet your horsepower needs.

ECS is the ideal partner for both compressor and pump installations or upgrades. Our total horsepower solution delivers shaft energy under a customized pricing structure.

Our structure adds value to your horsepower...

  • Long-term power pricing for horsepower delivery
  • Electric motor-driven horsepower package
  • Reliable access to the transmission grid
  • Flexible equipment financing
  • Maintenance services
Our total horsepower solution is structured to meet your process requirements, reduce your cost volatility, and increase the value of your business. By bundling these services together, we can provide a custom turnkey horsepower package that is reliable and economical. More importantly, as part of Enron, the world's leading marketer of electricity and natural gas, ECS has broad access to energy and commodities markets, capital resources, and infrastructure expertise to create value.
