In order to better develop and manage equity investment opportunities related to Enron's core businesses, Enron has created Enron Principal Investments.

Enron Principal Investments assumes the responsibility of investments previously found within Enron North America, Enron Broadband Services, Enron Investment Partners and the Enron Special Asset Group.


Enron Principal Investments works with all Enron business units to identify, execute, and manage equity investments that leverage Enron's unique and proprietary knowledge. These investments may be in the form of venture capital, leveraged buy-outs, traditional private equity and distressed debt positions.

Kevin Garland, Managing Director, heads this new business unit.
