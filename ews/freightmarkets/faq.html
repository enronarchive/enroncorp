Frequently Asked Questions

What is Enron Freight Markets all about?
Enron Freight Markets (EFM) was formed in March 2001. Our proven expertise in risk management and finance combined with our transportation services has enabled Enron to create innovative transportation solutions for shippers, carriers and third-party logistics companies.

What kind of value do you bring to the marketplace?
Enron Freight Markets adds value by providing reliable transportation service and offering customized risk management services to shippers, carriers and third party logistics companies.

Who is Enron?
Enron is one of the world's leading electricity, natural gas and communications companies. Enron manages efficient, flexible networks to reliably deliver physical products at predictable prices.
