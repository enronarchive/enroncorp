For Content Providers
Now you can finally deliver true, high bit rate streaming multimedia. Go ahead and create "power user" content and then distribute 30 frame per second content streams to your customers. Use Media Cast and the Enron Intelligent Network to deliver rich content to multiple targeted audiences simultaneously. With Media Cast, you gain a real advantage when it comes to streaming media, special events and other situations requiring even higher bandwidth feeds.

To help you manage your services, we provide online access to weekly usage statistics that can help identify demographic trends and customer usage characteristics. Statistics include total number of viewers, viewing details per file, viewing statistics by region (POP), viewing statistics by bandwidth and other stream activity information.

For Content Distributors
ISPs and D/C/ILECs, can now better differentiate services by offering richer media to subscribers without making large additional investments in a private network, a WAN, or in more servers. Using Media Cast over the Enron Intelligent Network, you reduce bandwidth costs, while gaining improved network performance, a new revenue stream and ongoing technical support. It all comes to you through Enron Broadband Services'-installed hardware and software, and our online resource area.

Service and Support
For content and service providers, Enron Broadband Services provides a 24x7 Network Operations Center and customer support line to ensure technical support is always available. We also provide an online resource where you can get updated information, manage your accounts, and receive online support.
