Management Team Bios

John L. Garrison, Jr.
John L. Garrison, Jr. is President and Chief Executive Officer of Azurix. He joined Azurix in April 1999 as a Managing Director, was named Executive Director in November 1999 and was promoted to Chief Operating Officer in March 2000. Prior to joining Azurix, Mr. Garrison served as Vice President and General Manager of the North American Agricultural Group of Case Corp. from August 1995 to April 1999. Before Joining Case Corp., Mr. Garrison served as a principal with Enron Development Corp. from July 1992 to July 1995 and was responsible for gas and power infrastructure development for the United States, Colombia and Mexico.

Colin F. Skellett
Colin F. Skellett is Vice Chairman, Operations of Azurix. Mr. Skellett served as Executive Director, Technical and Operating, Environmental and Safety Services of Azurix from October 1998 to October 1999. Mr. Skellett has served as Chief Executive of Wessex since January 1995. Mr. Skellett served as Managing Director of Wessex from September 1989 to January 1995. Mr. Skellett is also Chairman of Wessex Water Services Limited and is the U.K. representative on the European Union of National Associations of Water Suppliers and Wastewater Services.

Amanda K. Martin
Amanda K. Martin is President of North America for Azurix. From September 1998 to December 1999, Ms. Martin was Executive Director, Americas for Azurix. Commencing in 1991, Ms. Martin served in various positions with Enron Capital & Trade Resources Corp., including President of Energy and Finance Services from January 1998 to September 1998, Managing Director and Vice President of Business Ventures and Asset Management from September 1996 to January 1998, Vice President of Business Ventures and Asset Management from April 1994 to September 1996, and Senior Counsel from March 1993 to April 1994.

J. Michael Anderson
J. Michael Anderson is Chief Financial Officer of Azurix. Mr. Anderson joined Azurix in September 1999 and served as Senior Vice President of Corporate Development prior to accepting the CFO position. Before joining Azurix, Mr. Anderson spent ten years in the Global Mergers and Acquisitions Group of Chase Securities Inc., a subsidiary of the Chase Manhattan Corporation.
