GAS

Why will I receive 2 gas bills every month?
[Because ATCO Gas still owns and operates the pipelines and other equipment required to deliver and measure the natural gas you consume. They will continue to invoice for these services. Therefore, the invoice received from Enron Direct is only for the natural gas you actualy consume.]

Will our company save money by having Enron Direct supply our natural gas?
[Enron Direct provides competitive pricing via flexible contracts that reflect real-time natural gas pricing. The contracts can be short term or long term, i.e.1 to 5 years. While market prices are outside of our control we can reduce our customers exposure to market volatility be employing a variety of financial risk management tools.]

What if I have a service interruption?
[While Enron Direct can contact ATCO Gas on your behalf, we recommend that you call ATCO Gas directly.
ATCO Gas Customer Assistance Centre (Mon-Fri 7am-7pm): (403) 245-7888]

What if I have a gas emergency?
[Please contact ATCO Gas immediately at 24 Hour Emergency Service: (403) 245-7222 and then call Enron Direct toll free @ 1 (866) 656-0666].

Electricity

I had never noticed "Unaccounted For Energy" or "Losses" charges on my invoices before I switched to Enron Direct. Are these new charges?
[NO. You have always paid for these charges, but until deregulation, they were not itemized on your bill. For a full explanation of these and all of your charges, please see the reverse side of your invoice.]

I receive 2 bills for my natural gas, should I expect 2 bills for my electricity as well?
[NO. In the normal course, Enron Direct will invoice you for all the charges associated with your electricity supply.]

What if I have an electricity service interruption?
[During business hours (8am to 5 pm), please call Enron Direct and our Customer Service Team will forward your query to the appropriate team. After hours, contact your local Wires Service Provider directly. Their name and number can be found on the front of your Enron Direct invoice or in the front of your local Yellow Pages.]

What if I have an emergency?
[Please contact your Wire Services Provider immediately. The number for your Wire Service Provide can be found on the front of your invoice or in the front of your local Yellow Pages then call Enron Direct toll free @ 1 (866) 656-0666.]

GENERAL

When will Enron Direct be supplying products and services to residential consumers?
[Enron Direct is initially focused on providing a superior level of service to commercial customers in Alberta. We will be developing our residential strategy in the coming months.]

My business is located in a rural location. Is it possible to receive my invoices by fax or e-mail?
[ABSOLUTELY. Regardless of where your business resides, we are pleased to send you your invoices by fax, e-mail or mail.]

What if I have a question or concern about my Enron Direct invoice?
[There are a number of ways to contact us. 1) During business hours (8am to 5pm) you can call our Customer Service Team on 1-866-656-0666 or (403) 663-2800. 2) You may e-mail your query at inquiries@enrondirect.com. or 3) You can submit the "Customer query request" found on this web-site.]

When will my invoice payment be due?
[The due date for payment will be clearly indicated on your invoice.]
