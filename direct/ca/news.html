FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE: June 26, 2001

ENRON DIRECT ENTERS THE RETAIL ELECTRICITY AND NATURAL GAS MARKETS IN ALBERTA

ENRON DIRECT - AN ALTERNATIVE CHOICE FOR COMMERCIAL AND INDUSTRIAL BUSINESSES IN ALBERTA TO PURCHAS ELECTRICITY AND NATURAL GAS.

CALGARY -- Enron Direct Canada Corp. (Enron Direct), a subsidiary of Enron Corp. (NYSE:ENE) announced today that it has entered the retail electricity and natural gas markets in Alberta, targeting all commercial and light industrial businesses.

Alberta businesses, large and small, now have the freedom to choose Enron Direct for their electricity and natural gas requirements at competitive prices. Enron Direct provides predictable term pricing and reliable supply of electricity and natural gas. Enron Direct has the ability to leverage off the well-established business of its affiliate, Enron Canada Corp. (Enron Canada), the largest wholesale buyer and seller of electricity and natural gas in Canada. "We have the experience and commitment to give business consumers the price management necessary to reduce their financial exposure to fluctuating electricity and natural gas prices," said Rob Milnthorp, President & CEO of Enron Canada and Enron Direct.

"Enron Direct is excited to be offering all businesses across Alberta a choice of flexible and innovative contracts to help them better manage their electricity and natural gas costs. Enron Direct is an alternative to the existing affiliated incumbents - something that Alberta businesses have been calling for," said Darren Cross, Chief Operating Officer of Enron Direct.

"Enron Direct is assisting Calgary Co-op to control its costs in the power market by providing us with competitive pricing that enables us to better predict and manage our utility costs," stated Rod Peterson, property manager, Calgary Co-operative Limited.

Enron Corp. has been using the Enron Direct business model to supply commercial customers globally since 1993. Enron Direct has proven its ability to be competitive and offer energy alternatives for small and large businesses by providing an experienced and knowledgeable customer-orientated team of professionals to meet our customers' needs.

Enron Corp. is one of the world's leading electricity, natural gas and communications companies, delivering and managing commodity supplies around the globe. The company with revenues of US$101 billion in 2000, markets electricity and natural gas, delivers physical commodities and financial and risk management services to customers around the world and is developing an intelligent network platform to facilitate online business. Fortune magazine has named Enron Corp. "America's Most Innovative Company" for six consecutive years. Enron Corp.'s Internet address is www.enron.com. The stock is traded under the ticker symbol "ENE."
