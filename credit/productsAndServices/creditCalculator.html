Perform real-time, quantitative credit analysis based on Enron Cost of Credit (ECC) information using our Analytical Services.

In the Portfolios & Credit Information section of this website, you will find the Expected Loss Calculator. This calculator will allow you to input your credit exposure and determine your Expected Loss based on ECC data, the Expected Loss shows how much a particular credit exposure is costing your business. You can assess the Expected Loss associated with one company or across an entire portfolio.

 
  With our Analytical Services, you can :
     Quantify credit risk, rather than rely on subjective, qualitative information
     Assess the credit risk of a specific company or analyze your entire portfolio
     Make faster decisions about specific transactions
    
Perform "what if" scenarios to see how making specific changes to your portfolio affects your overall credit risk

Other analytical tools are in the final stages of development and will be released in the third quarter of 2001. These easy-to-use, interactive tools will allow you to analyze credit information, such as the Value at Risk (VaR), on a specific company or across your entire portfolio. In addition, you will be able to perform a variety of other calculations so you can make faster, better credit decisions.
