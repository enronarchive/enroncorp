Access real-time credit information on thousands of companies worldwide. Once you've registered on our website, you will find market-driven credit information on over 10,000 companies. You have the option to view this information directly on our website or have it delivered to you via the Enron Credit RiskSheet.

Our primary informational tool is the Enron Cost of Credit ("ECC"). The ECC is essentially a "price" for credit risk, derived using Enron Credit's proprietary model. You can monitor ECC information for specific industry sectors by referring to our credit indices. When the ECC for a specific company changes, you can be notified via price alerts.
The ECC can be easily incorporated into your existing credit analysis processes. The ECC can assist you in :
       Comparing credit risks of potential customers, suppliers and business partners
       Pricing new deals and business opportunities to account for credit risk
       Setting dynamic credit limits
       Hedging when necessary
In addition to the ECC, members of Enron Credit have access to other valuable credit information including the estimated probability of bankruptcy, estimated recovery rates, and Digital Bankruptcy Swap prices. Like the ECC, these prices are market-driven and updated daily.
Registered users can also create custom portfolios to track credit data for specific companies, industry sectors or geographic region, Please click here to view a sample portfolio.

If you would like to have your portfolio data sent to you in the form of a simple spreadsheet on a daily, weekly, or monthly basis, consider subscribing to the Enron Credit RiskSheet.
