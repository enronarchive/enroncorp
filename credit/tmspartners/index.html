The Enron Credit Network
The Enron Credit Network is made up of industry leaders who complement Enron Credit's information and hedging products with top-quality technology, infrastructure and consulting products and services.
Reuters Credit

Reuters Credit acts as an aggregator of credit information pulling together Reuters Information's proprietary database of content and combining it with the credit data of its content partners including JP Morgan, Moody's, Riskmetrics and Enron Credit. The Reuters Credit service is tailored to serve the needs of asset managers, issuers, analysts, researchers and other finance professionals.

Enron Credit users who subscribe to Reuters Credit can move seamlessly between the two due to a synchronised password process.

ERisk

ERisk is a full-service provider of strategic enterprise risk management solutions, ERisk has made ERM accessible in a scalable and cost-effective form. Founded in 1999 as a venture of Oliver, Wyman & Company, they work with financial services organizations, property & casualty insurers, energy companies and non-financial corporations.

ERisk provides their clients with a unique combination of risk management expertise and analytics. ERisk has the tools you need to transform risk management processes, build management effectiveness, reduce losses and increase shareholder value.
