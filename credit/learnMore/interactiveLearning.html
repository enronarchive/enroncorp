To help you learn more about corporate credit risk, Enron Credit is developing a series of interactive learning modules. These modules should help you understand corporate credit risk and the various tools you can use to mitigate that risk. We currently have two learning modules available on our site:

   Identifying Counterparty Bankruptcy Risk - See how specific business situations are affected by the bankruptcy of a business counterpart and what companies can do to protect themselves
   Product Comparison - Compare credit risk mitigation tools including Digital Bankruptcy Swaps, Letters of Credit, Credit Insurance, and Credit Default Swaps

If there is a specific topic you would like to see in a future interactive learning module, please contact us with your ideas.
