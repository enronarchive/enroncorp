The Power of Enron
Enron is one of the world's leading electricity, natural gas and communications companies with approximately $101 billion in revenues in 2000.

In addition to being the premier marketer of electricity and natural gas, Enron also delivers physical commodities as well as financial and risk management services to customers around the world. Its retail business provides energy risk management solutions for small to very large business customers worldwide. In today's globally-connected world, businesses depend on high-performing communications and Enron has a developed the premier intelligent network platform to facilitate online business and high-bandwidth content delivery. Additionally, through innovative technology such as its web-based commodity trading system, Enron Online, it is reaching a greater number of customers at a lower cost than ever. In 2000, Enron Online executed 548,000 transactions with a $336 billion notional value and is now the world's largest web-based eCommerce system.
