IGNORING ENERGY RISK IS NO LONGER AN OPTION.
Enron can help.

There are two proven hedges against volatile electricity expenses - reduced consumption and progressive, strategically managed purchasing of electricity. Enron provides expertise in both areas. We look at all factors at risk and identify opportunities to turn our customer's energy risk into a business advantage - from reducing our customers' consumption with energy efficiency projects to leveraging value through our extensive energy trading capability.

By effectively tailoring an all-inclusive energy management plan that includes commodity management, energy asset management, monitoring performance, and capital solutions to meet each customer's needs, Enron can turn today's market volatility and uncertainty to the client's advantage. Being passive about your energy is no longer an option. After all, a disappointing earnings report is not how you want to learn you've been wasting energy.
