Jobfinder

Logging on to Enron's Jobfinder

If you've never logged on to eCandidate before, click SIGN ME UP! All you need to do is enter a user name and a password. Then, you'll be able to create a profile and submit your resume when you find a job that interests you.
Creating a Profile

The first time you log on, you'll need to create a personal profile. It's easy - just fill in your name, address, and phone numbers. You can also enter information about your work experience, your education, and your references. You can even store your resume in eCandidate. You can update your profile anytime: just log on, update your information, and click SAVE PROFILE.
Searching for Jobs

You can search for jobs at any time - you don't have to log on first. However, if you want to apply for the jobs that interest you, you will need to log on and create a personal profile. You can add jobs to your personal list of interesting jobs and click APPLY ALL to apply for all the jobs in your list at once.
Filling Out Job Forms or Custom Forms

After you apply for a job, the company may want more information from you. If they do, you'll see a special page when you log on that lists any forms the company would like you to fill out. To send your answers to the company, just select the form from the list and fill it out.
Logging Off
Remember to log off from eCandidate at the end of your session to protect your personal information. If you forget to log off, eCandidate will log you off automatically.
