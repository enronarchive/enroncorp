Trading Track

Enron's Trading Track is a unique program aimed at those who have already made the decision that they want trading and risk management as a career. The 18-24 month program is tailored to produce the most effective traders in the industry. Employees in this Houston-based track will have the opportunity to experience and learn from the operations of several trading-related areas within Enron.

Rotations average six months in length and span the areas of:

  • 24-hour trading
  • Risk Management
  • Structuring
  • Logistics
  • Fundamentals

Participants will gain experience with various commodities as well as the complex deal structures we use to serve our customers.

As with any of our positions, you'll fit in best with the Trading Track if you're a very smart, self-starting, and creative team player who can thrive on constant change and risk, and even occasional chaos. Here at Enron you're surrounded by the most innovative individuals that the market has offer. The Trading Track gives you the chance to advance your skills by learning from leaders of the risk management and trading industry.

We're seeking candidates with a background in finance, accounting, engineering, or other industry-related fields. If you've got the trading instinct and the willingness to stretch your abilities, the Enron Trading Track may be the place for you.
