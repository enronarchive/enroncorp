Frequently Asked Questions

Does Enron have a direct stock purchase plan?
Yes. The transfer agent offers holders of Enron Corp. Common stock the opportunity to purchase additional shares of common stock by participating in the DirectSERVICE Program. Additional shares can be purchased with cash payments of as little as $25 after an initial investment of $250 for new shareholders with a limit of $120,000 per calendar year.

Does Enron pay dividends?
Yes. Enron pays a quarterly dividend.

Does Enron have a DRIP plan and if so, how do I enroll?
Yes. Thru the DirectSERVICE Program, shareholders have the opportunity to reinvest part or all of their dividends.
