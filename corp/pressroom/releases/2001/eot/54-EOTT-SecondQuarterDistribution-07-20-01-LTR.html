EOTT ENERGY PARTNERS, L.P. DECLARES QUARTERLY CASH DISTRIBUTION

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE: Friday, July 20, 2001

HOUSTON -- EOTT Energy Partners, L.P. (the Partnership) announced today a distribution of $0.475 per common unit for the second quarter of 2001, or $1.90 on an annualized basis. The second quarter distribution is payable August 14, 2001, to unitholders of record as of July 31, 2001.

EOTT Energy Partners, L.P. is a major independent marketer and transporter of crude oil in North America. EOTT transports most of the lease crude oil it purchases via pipeline, which includes 8,200 miles of active intrastate and interstate pipeline and gathering systems, and a fleet of 285 owned or leased trucks. In addition, EOTT owns and operates a hydrocarbon processing plant; a natural gas liquids storage facility; and a 240-mile liquid pipeline grid system. EOTT Energy Corp., a wholly-owned subsidiary of Enron Corp., is the general partner of EOTT Energy Partners, L.P. with headquarters in Houston. EOTT’s Internet address is www.eott.com. The Partnership’s Common Units are traded on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker symbol "EOT."
