ENRON GLOBAL POWER & PIPELINES L.L.C. ANNOUNCES FIRST QUARTER DIVIDEND

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE: Tuesday, February 18, 1997

HOUSTON -- The Board of Directors of Enron Global Power & Pipelines L.L.C. (Ticker symbol: EPP) today declared a first quarter dividend of $0.25 per share, payable March 14, 1997 to shareholders of record February 28, 1997. The indicated annual rate is $1.00 per share.

EPP's assets consist of interests in two power plants in the Philippines, power plants in Guatemala and the Dominican Republic and natural gas pipeline systems in Argentina and Colombia.

Enron Global Power & Pipelines, majority owned by ENRON, is traded on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker symbol, "EPP."
