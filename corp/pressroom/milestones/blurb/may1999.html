The 826 MW Phase I of the Dabhol Power Project begins commercial operation, and financing for the 1,624 MW Phase II and India's first LNG receiving facility is completed. When Phase II construction is completed in late 2001, Dabhol will become the world's largest independent natural gas-fired power facility.
