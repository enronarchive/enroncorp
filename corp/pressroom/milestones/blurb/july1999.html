Enron announces Azurix initial public offering.

The 3,000-kilometer Bolivia-to-Brazil natural gas pipeline, one of the largest gas projects ever undertaken in South America, begins commercial operation. The pipeline system has a capacity of 30 million cubic meters per day.

Enron sells its interest in Enron Oil & Gas, but retains its China and India assets.
