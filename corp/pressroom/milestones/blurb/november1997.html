Northern Natural Gas initiates a major market-wide expansion project, Peak Day 2000, a five-year effort that increases the pipeline's contracted capacity by 350,000 million cubic feet of gas per day. Today, this brings Northern's total Peak Day capacity to 4.3 billion cubic feet of gas per day (Bcf/d) compared to 2.8 Bcf/d in 1988.
