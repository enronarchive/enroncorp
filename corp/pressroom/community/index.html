In the Community

Just as Enron is leading the way in developing new energy markets, we're taking a leading role in our communities. This means more than just making contributions to worthy causes. It means providing the energy for positive change using our most precious asset - Enron people. Each year, over 10,000 employees dedicate thousands of hours to a variety of community-inspired, needs-driven programs. And it means developing programs that promote healthy families, education and a clean environment.
