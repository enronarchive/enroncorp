Education

Enron has a tremendous commitment to education. It funds programs which aid students from kindergarten through college.

Prescription for Reading*
Enron is promoting literacy among 30,000 Houston-area preschool children with this innovative program. more

Technology for All (TFA)*
Enron, EBS, and TFA are teaming up to bridge the digital divide by providing technology access and educational resources to disadvantaged children. more

Rise Schools of Texas*
The Rise Schools of Texas are private schools providing the highest quality of early childhood education for children with Down Syndrome and similar developmental delays, as well as children without developmental delays. more

Community 101sm*
Students learn about community needs through a high school course that teaches the importance of giving. more

Higher Education
Enron is a firm believer in the importance of higher education, providing over $2.7 million annually for scholarships, grants, and other gifts to institutions nationwide. more
