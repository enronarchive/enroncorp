United Way

Through Enron's Matching Gifts program, the company and employees pledge more than $3 million annually to the United Way and 82 of its member agencies in the greater Houston area. In 1998, Enron received the prestigious "Summit Award" from the United Way in recognition of our outstanding support.

Each year Enron employees adopt a United Way agency. The relationship is based on volunteerism, not on corporate contribution dollars. Ripley House is Enron's 1999 United Way adopted agency. The Boys and Girls Clubs of Greater Houston was the 1998 adopted agency, and Search Homeless Project was the 1997 adopted agency.

We welcome your suggestions for next year's adopted agency. Please contact community relations.
