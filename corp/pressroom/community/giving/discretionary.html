Discretionary Grants

Discretionary grants enable Enron to support a variety of organizations that are working to improve the communities where our employees, customers, and colleagues live and work. Enron's community investments range from supporting programs that promote cultural diversity and sponsoring art exhibits to contributing dollars for international disaster relief efforts and funding endowments at private institutions.

Enron requests all grant proposals in writing at least 3 months prior to proposed funding needs. Please send proposals to:

Enron Community Relations
1400 Smith Street
Houston, TX 77002
Attn: Community Development
