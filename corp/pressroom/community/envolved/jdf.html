Juvenile Diabetes Foundation

Since 1991, Enron employees have been strong supporters of the Juvenile Diabetes Foundation (JDF) annual Walk to Cure Diabetes. We have consistently sponsored one of the top JDF walking teams in Texas and the nation, and our fundraising efforts have earned national recognition by JDF.
