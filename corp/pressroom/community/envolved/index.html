Enron Envolved

While monetary and in-kind gifts are important, hands-on volunteers are the lifeblood of community agencies. Over the years, employee volunteers have provided helping hands, intellectual capital, and skilled labor to non-profit agencies.

Enron is committed to supporting our employee-driven volunteer initiatives. Through Enron EnvolvedSM, our volunteer program, employees work in many different ways to serve our communities. Enron works closely with community leaders to provide opportunities to apply their talents, energy, and enthusiasm.
