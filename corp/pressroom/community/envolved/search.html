SEARCH

The Houston SEARCH homeless center receives volunteer time and individual contributions from dozens of Enron employees on a variety of projects. These projects include renovating the House of Tiny Treasures (the only registered, licensed childcare facility in Houston for children of homeless families), providing business consulting, and creating a coin drive that raised more than $600,000 for SEARCH in three years.

Change Houston is a community-based campaign designed to raise awareness of homelessness in Houston. Taking place in corporate and community groups throughout the city, this campaign gives everyone a chance to be a part of the solution to homelessness. If everyone gives just $5 in 5 days, we can help Houston's homeless get back on their feet.

Any individual can participate. Drop off your spare change, dollars, or checks at the Change Houston house located in the lobby of the Enron building starting in November.

Five days. Five dollars. This may not seem like a lot, but last year our volunteers brought in nearly $200,000!

SEARCH welcomes donations of food and clothing. Donations are accepted 24 hours a day at the SEARCH building located at 2505 Fannin, Houston, TX 77002. Please visist their web site at www.searchproject.org to learn more about this exciting charity.
