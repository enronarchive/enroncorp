Sunshine Kids

The Sunshine Kids Foundation began in Houston in 1982 as a non-profit organization designed to provide positive group activities and emotional support for children who are diagnosed with cancer. The foundation, assisted by volunteers, provides a variety of sports and cultural activities, free of charge, for children who are receiving cancer treatments in hospitals across the United States. Each year, Enron sponsors two programs with the Sunshine Kids—Spring Fling and Sunshine Kids Fall Getaway. Enron volunteers also participate in special projects throughout the year.
