Ronald McDonald House

Many families travel to large medical centers to get the best treatment for their children. Ronald McDonald provides a safe, comfortable place for a family to stay as they face the trauma of their child's illness. The whole family can stay in the House at a very nominal charge while the child is being diagnosed and treated for an illness such as cancer, leukemia, heart disease or an organ transplant.

Every other Thursday, Enron volunteers prepare dinner for Ronald McDonald House families and plan activities for visiting children.

The Ronald McDonald House of Houston is located at 1907 Holcombe Boulevard, Houston, Texas 77030. The phone number is (713) 795-3500.

Visit the Ronald McDonald House web site at www.ronaldmcdonald-houston.org.
