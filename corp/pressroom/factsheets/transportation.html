Fact Sheet: Enron Transportation Services

Business Overview

Enron Transportation Services (ETS) encompasses Enron's pipeline group and transportation services. With approximately 25,000 miles of interstate and intrastate pipeline, ETS operates one of the country's largest natural gas transmission systems and transports 15 percent of all natural gas consumed in the United States. ETS comprises one of the nation's largest natural gas pipeline systems in terms of throughput and miles of pipe.
