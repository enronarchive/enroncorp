The ePowerTM Media Productions Streaming Solution

ePower Media Productions offers complete and custom solutions for the delivery of streaming multimedia content over the Enron Intelligent Network.

We are a global one-stop-shop to out-source all the components associated with Web casting - streaming live or on-demand content. ePower Media Productions manages the production and execution of the Web cast from concept to completion, and manages a network of regional preferred vendors as necessary to facilitate the best solutions for customers. Replicating content across the intelligent Enron Intelligent Network using the ePowered Media Cast distribution platform, ePower Media Productions produces a superior quality streaming rich-media experience. Providing you with the reliability and customer service that you expect, ePower Media Productions will Web cast your events from end to end.

Webcast an event.

Why Webcast?
ePower Media Productions Web casts offer customers the ability to deliver their message quickly and efficiently with the ability to reach many simultaneous viewers. Crossing the boundaries of geography and time, Web casting allows viewers around the world to watch events live or on-demand--a truly expedient and economic solution for delivering your multi-media content to desktops world wide. Using the intelligent Enron Intelligent Network, providers can be assured that they will be broadcasting high-quality viewing experiences of streaming rich media to their target viewers on any networked device.

What to Webcast?
The following list provides some examples of streaming content suitable to live and on-demand Web casting:

  • Corporate meetings and announcements
  • Distance learning and training
  • Roadshows and research reports
  • News events
  • Marketing, promotions and advertising
  • Product launches
  • Sports events
  • Pay-per-view events
  • Concerts
  • Motion pictures
