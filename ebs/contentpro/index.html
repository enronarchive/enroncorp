What we offer content providers
Quite simply, we provide you with the ability to deliver your content directly to your customers, the way it was meant to be seen. Our suite of ePowered application services brings TV quality video and stereophonic sound straight to the desktop, dramatically improving the viewing experience for consumers and increasing the value of your video streaming assets. We work seamlessly behind the scenes to enhance the performance of your video content by deploying one of the world's most advanced next-generation Internet networking solutions, purely dedicated to intense broadband content. Meanwhile, you increase the "stickiness" of your Web site, generating greater ROI on your advertising and eBusiness initiatives as you extend the power of your online brand.

With ePowered applications, your content is streamed to thirsty, sophisticated powerful end-user constituencies with these unique differences:

  • Quality: We have deployed the most advanced optical networking solutions from leading technology innovators such as Ciena, Cisco Systems, and Sun Microsystems. Combined with our cutting-edge Broadband Operating System, this solution set completes what we call the Enron Intelligent Network, the most dynamic, robust broadband network platform on the planet.

  • Reach: Our wide ranging distribution relationships with ISP's, CLEC's, and RBOC partners gives us the most extensive reach of any network provider today, and brings your content to an ever-growing number of broadband enabled viewers. View the latest list of ePowered Broadband Delivery partners.

  • Security: Our Extranet-enabled ePowered reporting tools, provide you online hit statistics such as how many viewers are watching your streams, per encoding rate, per geographical region. This mission critical information enables you to drive demand for advertising on your web site, as well as forecast future programming and eBusiness requirements.

  • Delivery: Enron Broadband Services also offers a wide range of bandwidth transport solutions that enable businesses to scale capacity quickly and dynamically in order to handle your increasing traffic needs. Imagine being able to plan a new streaming event with enough capacity and servers to meet the growing demand for your multimedia communications.

You invest too much time & money producing cutting-edge content to leave the choice of delivery to an unpredictable network solution. Get ePowered, and realize the advantages delivering your premium broadband content on the most dynamic, next-generation Internet network solution available today.

To ensure the success of our partners, we offer several innovative, helpful programs and other resources to increase the effectiveness of your ePowered applications. Learn more about our resources for Content Providers.
