The broadband explosion is real, it is here now, and it will fundamentally change the existing Internet delivery platform. The public Internet was designed for the text world, not the delivery of rich media. Enron Broadband Services Inc. is delivering the platform for the emerging net economy, to bypass the Internet with point-to-point connections. The Enron Intelligent Network (EIN™) and the ePower™ content it delivers will change the way you think about doing business on the Web. The Enron business model, which emphasizes open, robust networks and markets, will enable Enron to develop the most efficient broadband delivery network in the world.

We are rapidly building our Enron Intelligent Network—a national fiber backbone and data network—and furnishing this network with advanced, ePower™ content solutions for sale via our channel partners. By the end of this year, our backbone will cover approximately 20,000 miles and will carry data, video and multimedia content at speeds up to 10 gigabits-per-second (OC-192).

We serve carriers like Inter Exchange Carriers (IXCs), Regional Bell Operating Companies (RBOCs), Local Exchange Carriers (LECs), wireless data network providers and Internet Service Providers (ISPs). Enron Broadband Services serves major content providers and end users who expect more from the Internet. Our ePower™ content solutions are delivered over the most flexible, high-speed, reliable and fault tolerant network available, the Enron Intelligent Network.
