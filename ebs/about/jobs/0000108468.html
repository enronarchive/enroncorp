SR TAX SPECIALIST

ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS: Responsible for supporting worldwide tax reporting for Enron Broadband Services including income tax compliance, FAS 109 reporting and tax components of operating plans and current estimates. Activities include review of U.S. Forms 1120, 1065 and 5471, review of state income tax filings, supporting corporate federal and state audit groups as required and preparation and review of monthly and year end tax accruals. Interaction with accounting groups to assure proper reporting to support tax requirements will be required.

ESSENTIAL REQUIREMENTS: Bachelor's or Master's degree in Accounting and CPA. Minimum of 4 years corporate tax experience, with emphasis in corporate reporting and analysis.

PREFERRED SKILLS: NA.

SPECIAL CHARACTERISTICS: Strong organizational, analytical and systems skills are essential. Demonstrated ability to effectively communicate orally and in writing at all levels within the organization. Ability to handle multiple assignments and prioritize projects. Must be a team player and a self-starter.
