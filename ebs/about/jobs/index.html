Enron Broadband Services, Inc., part of the Enron Corp. family of businesses based in Portland, Oregon, is an innovative communications services company. Our employees keep us on the leading edge. If you have the right blend of Internet, telecommunications, and business applications knowledge and experience, can think "outside the box" and thrive in a fast-paced, innovative environment, we invite you to consider our current openings.

View a current list of Enron Broadband Services, Inc. job postings.

How to apply: To be considered for a position, you must submit an application. Download an application, or contact us at ECI_staffing@enron.net to submit your resume, request a copy of the position description and/or to request a formal application in the mail. In addition, you may mail or fax your request or documents to us along with your resume. Please note, Enron Broadband Services does not accept unsolicited resumes/applications. Be sure to indicate your name, return address, and the position for which you are applying. Our mailing address is:

Human Resources Department
Enron Broadband Services, Inc.
2100 SW River Parkway, Suite 800
Portland, OR 97201
Fax: 503-886-0436

No agency referrals please. Applicants for positions with Enron Broadband Services Inc. may be subject to skill and/or aptitude, physical and psychological tests, and background investigations. In addition, they will be subject to a drug analysis for illicit drugs.
