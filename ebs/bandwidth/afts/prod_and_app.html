Applications for Wavelength Services

Enron's Wavelength Services offer you a flexible, future network.

Wavelength Services is the ideal way to accommodate your company's following needs:

  • Immediate network for start up companies
  • Transparently passing a SONET ring
  • Additional capacity on constrained route
  • Backbone upgrades instead of costly network augmentation
  • Ring closure without a network build
  • Diverse routing capabilities
  • Alternative or additional route to existing rings
  • Shorten a SONET ring
  • Capacity expansion for an ISP

Potential customers:

  • Small to medium sized providers currently operating at multiple network capacities.
  • Large facility based IXCs wanting to upgrade their backbone, close rings, offer diverse routing, or add another route
  • ISPs who have large data networks and want to expand their capacity
  • LEC, CLEC, and IXC entities
  • Large corporate customers
