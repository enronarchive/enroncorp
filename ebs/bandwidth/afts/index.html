Picture a world free of bandwidth constraints. A world where you are able to envision what tomorrow's network will require and you are prepared to meet those requirements.

Introducing Wavelength Services from Enron Broadband Services. Wavelength Services is a leased, dense wave channel of capacity on Enron’s Dense Wave Division Multiplexing (DWDM) network. With Wavelength Services, an increase in your network capacity and functionality doesn’t mean an increase in build and maintenance costs. Wavelength Services is faster than constructing your own network and more cost effective than equipping and maintaining dark fiber.

With the new network capabilities offered by Wavelength Services, the quality of your network isn't compromised by the ever increasing demands on network capacity and clarity. With bandwidth flexibility like never before, carriers can mix SONET/SDH, ATM and IP traffic on a common optical network while eliminating the intervening synchronous layer on ATM and FAST IP networks.

Enron's Wavelength Services allows us to offer virtually unlimited bandwidth potential. A partnership with Enron's Wavelength Services product offers multiple opportunities for network capacity expansion, diverse routing, ring completion and more global presence without expanding the resources and time involoved with construction and mainenance.
