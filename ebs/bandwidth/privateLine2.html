Private Line Solutions provide you with a secure, private network for data, voice, and video delivery.

Description

Enron offers a variety of longhaul, SONET capacity levels to give you the flexibility to only purchase the amount of capacity that your business needs. Also, Enron will offer you a scalable solution that will allow you to migrate to higher capacity as your network needs increase.

With Private Line Solutions from Enron, you can choose DS-3 (45 Mbps), OC-3 (155 Mbps), OC-12 (622 Mbps), and OC-48 (2.5 Gbps) level services. Both channelized and clear channel services are available.

Applications

Private Line Solutions can accommodate multiple capacity needs. Some examples of these are:

  • Provide backbone network to deliver data, voice, and/or video delivery between two company office locations
  • Link multiple company office locations to the central headquarters for data delivery
  • Connect ATM switches for an ATM network
  • Link voice switches to establish a voice network
