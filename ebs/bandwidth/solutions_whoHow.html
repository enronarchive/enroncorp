 Bandwidth Solutions:
 Who Benefits and How?

EBS's Bandwidth Solutions allows market participants to take control of their price exposure. Consumers gain flexibility and virtual on-demand bandwidth and sellers gain better control of their network assets in a volatile market.

Owners/Sellers of bandwidth, such as Network Service Providers, can take advantage of Enron's Bandwidth Solutions in one of several ways:

  1. Secure Future Demand
    When a network service provider embarks on new network build-outs, it asks it self; how best do I secure capacity? How do I finance network build-outs? How can I rationalize my decision to acquire network assets? Enron's Bandwidth management products give network providers the ability to sell today bandwidth to be delivered in the future. Selling forward large amounts of bandwidth through a commodity market gives the seller guaranteed future cash flow, lowers the seller's cost of capital and smoothes future earnings.
  2. Salvage a Perishable Asset
    Bandwidth, like airline seats and hotel rooms, is perishable. Lit circuits not sold by network service providers represent a potentially enormous opportunity cost. With marginal costs approaching zero, the incremental revenue gained by selling "last minute" bandwidth is pure profit. Although much time is spent estimating supply and demand along a network, planning is never perfect. There is often unexpected excess capacity or unexpected excess customer opportunity.
  3. Create Operational Redundancy
    Network reliability is paramount. "Best efforts" will no longer suffice with bandwidth customers increasingly needing near perfect QoS. Bandwidth risk management and trading affords service providers the ability to build network redundancy on a near real time basis. The ability to react to an unplanned shortage, e.g. back hoe fiber cut or demand spike, allows end customers to remain connected while redundant bandwidth at the wholesale level is acquired in a near real-time fashion.

Users/Buyers of bandwidth can benefit from Enron's Bandwidth Solutions in the following ways:
  1. Obtain Price and Volume Flexibility
    Enron's Bandwidth Solutions minimize the hassles of bandwidth procurement, providing a flexible and efficient method of buying and selling bandwidth products. Bandwidth users that are locked into long-term bandwidth contracts often find themselves paying above market rates for their bandwidth. EBS will pay these companies cash today for the opportunity to sell them bandwidth in the future. These companies can sell forward contracts to recoup much of their investment. Alternatively, if a user realizes that they have purchased excess bandwidth, they can sell forward this extra capacity.
  2. Bandwidth On-demand
    The ability to purchase and procure bandwidth on short notice for short periods of time empowers users to accurately plan the capacity, location, and use of bandwidth. Whether it is the streaming video image of a CEO addressing her company offices across the globe or B2B extranets, short-term on-demand bandwidth can address these assorted demand spikes. An enterprise's ability to quickly purchase bandwidth for short periods with confidence in short provisioning intervals provides an enormous amount of value. Similarly, network builders in need of short-term capacity until their own build is completed will benefit from bandwidth on-demand.
