  Dark Fiber:
 Description
 Availability

Enron’s Dark Fiber offers a flexible solution for your network needs.

Contract Term

Enron’s Dark Fiber standard contract term is a 20 year IRU. An IRU is an Indefeasible Right of Use which means that the customer will have an unrestricted indefeasible right to use the IRU fibers. At the end of the IRU term, Enron will transfer the title of the fiber. Short term leases are available on an individual case basis.

Technical Specifications

Enron cable is buried with a minimum cover of 4 feet. The conduit contains two types of fiber, Lucent TrueWave RS and SMF, to give you the flexibility to determine what type of fiber is best for your network needs.

Maintenance

EBS provides basic maintenance service for the system, the IRU fibers and any common equipment on the system. The customer will incur additional fees if they purchase this service from Enron.

Collocation

EBS offers collocation for Dark Fiber customers in EBS POPs, Regeneration, and OAMP sites. An Enron site is typically every 40 to 50 miles. Depending on the specific site, either a locked caged space or a locked cabinet space will be available for Dark Fiber customers.
