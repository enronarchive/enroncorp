The capacity needs for your business are increasing and you need to be confident that your information will reach its destination. If you are concerned about the cost of a private network but don't trust the unreliability of a shared network, we have a solution for you.

Consider Private Line Solutions from Enron Broadband Services. Enron has multiple levels of capacity available to meet your capacity needs at a price you cannot ignore. Enron offers your business the flexibility to lease Time Division Multiplexing or SONET Service with Enron's Private Line Services or a dense wave channel of capacity on our DWDM network with Enron's Wavelength Services.

Enron's Private Line Solutions will provide a longhaul channel of capacity between two longhaul Points of Presence (POPs). The channel of capacity is dedicated to your company to ensure a secure flow of information. The capacity can be used as your backbone network to accommodate your growing network needs.
