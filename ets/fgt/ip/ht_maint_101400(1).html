To: All Florida Gas Transmission Customers

Re: HotTap System Maintenance

 

FGT would like to notify our customers that we are extending the normal HotTap maintenance window hours beginning Saturday, October 14, 2000.

The current Hotap maintenance window schedule is Sunday through Friday 1:00 a.m. to 3:00 a.m., and Saturdays from 10:30 p.m. to 3:00 a.m.

Beginning October 14, 2000, the new HotTap maintenance window hours will be as follows:

    • Sunday through Friday 11:30 p.m. to 3:00 a.m.

    • Saturdays - 10:30 p.m to 3:00 a.m. (no change)

Please Note:

  • During normal system maintenance TMS will be unavailable. EDI transactions such as nominations, quick response, and capacity release will be processed after the system returns to normal operations at 3:00 a.m.

  • All times are Central Clock Time (CCT).
