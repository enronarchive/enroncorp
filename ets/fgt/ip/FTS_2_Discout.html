April 18, 2001

 

FGT’s Phase IV Expansion is nearing completion and will be in service on May 1, 2001 as anticipated. Pursuant to the Stipulation and Agreement in Docket Nos. CP99-94-000 and RP96-366-000, et. al., filed with the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission ("FERC") on June 2, 1999 ("Settlement"), all shippers which both (1) held permanent FTS-2 capacity as of the date of the Settlement or who have entered into an FTS-2 Service Agreement to become effective upon the in-service date of the Phase IV Expansion, and (2) either supported or did not oppose the Settlement will receive certain rate caps resulting in discounts commencing on the in-service date of the expansion.

The specific provisions regarding the Phase IV rate caps and discounts can be found in Section II. Rate Provisions of the Settlement. The parties eligible for these rate caps and/or discounts are identified on Attachment A to the Settlement.

FGT’s web site and rate cards will continue to reflect the FERC approved maximum tariff rates applicable to Rate Schedule FTS-2 (reservation 76.18¢/dth; usage 1.42¢/dth), but you will be billed at the discounted rates pursuant to the Settlement.
