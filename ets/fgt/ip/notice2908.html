NEW CAPACITY RELEASE RULES MANDATED BY ORDER 637

Effective May 23, 2000 
Revised:  May 26, 2000

Please note that recent FERC orders have clarified Order 637 concerning 
capacity release transactions as follows:

1.	Initial release to prearranged party for 31 days or less would NOT 
require posting/bidding, regardless of rate.

2.	Subsequent release to same prearranged party for 31 days or less WOULD 
require posting/bidding, regardless of rate, unless 28 days or more have 
elapsed from the expiration of previous release.

3.	Releases for greater than one month, but less than one year, WOULD 
require posting/bidding, regardless of rate.

4.      Releases for one year or greater would:  
           (a) require posting/bidding if less than max rate; and
           (b) not allow for posting/bidding if at max rates. 


Note:  The phrase  regardless of rate  stated above include: (a)  less than 
max rate, (b) at max rate and (c) above max rate.
