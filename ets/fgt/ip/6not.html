         due to significant improvement in gas quality effective the 1st of 

         February 2001.
  
         POINT NAME (DRN#s)               TOTAL RECEIPT
         Enron Sabine Pass Plant          120,000 MMBtu/d
         (23422)

          
    FGT continues to receive gas in Zone 1, 2, and 3 which
    does not meet its quality standards, under Section 2(A)(1)
    of the General Terms and Conditions of its FERC Gas Tariff,
    Volume No. I, in that the gas contains high concentrations
    of hexane and heavier components which are causing
    interference with the proper operation of FGT facilities.

    In light of total market demand, such objectionable gas
    can no longer be blended so as to eliminate the operational
    problems.  Therefore, FGT is limiting total receipts at the
    following interconnects, beginning the gas day of December 9th
    and until further notice:

    POINT NAME (DRN#s)               TOTAL RECEIPT

    EGP-Cow Island Plant             10,000 MMBtu/d
    (28166)

    Neumin Production - Moss Bluff    5,000 MMBtu/d
    (287470)

    Neumin No. Vinton/FGT             5,000 MMBtu/d
    (258452)

    
        Additional restrictions may be necessary.

   There are alternative receipt points located in all three zones, 
   many in Zone 3, which have no Hexane+ problems at this time.  
    
   FGT will advise when increased volumes can be scheduled at
   the above locations.  FGT anticipates these circumstances to
   continue until certain processing plants become operational again.
