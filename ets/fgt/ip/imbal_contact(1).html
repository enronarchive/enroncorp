To: All FGT Customers

RE: Imbalance Trading Contacts

 

Customers wishing to trade imbalances have made a number of requests of Florida Gas Transmission Company ("FGT") for the name and telephone number of a contact for those companies which appear on FGT's monthly posting of imbalances ("balancing documents").

FGT currently posts by the 10th of each month the customer name and imbalance for the previous month. FGT is willing to assist those customers who wish to trade imbalances by adding to this posting the name and telephone number of a contact person. If you would like FGT to post a contact for you when you have an imbalance, please forward the name and telephone number of the contact to hilda.lindley@enron.com.
