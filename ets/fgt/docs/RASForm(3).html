Access to the FGT Emergency Remote Access Server (RAS)

 

Florida Gas Transmission (FGT) offers an emergency dial up application to our HotTap system for customers who need assistance in electronically submitting information via the Internet to FGT. This service will allow you to access the Throughput Management System (TMS) during emergency connectivity problems.

To access the FGT Emergency Remote Access Server, please do the following:

  1. Notify the HotTap Helpdesk at 713-853-6063 or 1-800-421-6221 for any immediate assistance needed.
  2. Complete the following form and return such form to the HotTap Helpdesk at hottap@enron.com for our records. Please note that the passwords provided pursuant to this emergency access service are for one day access only.
