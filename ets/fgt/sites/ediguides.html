Electronic Data Interchange

One of the ways we can conduct business with our customers is via Electronic Data Interchange (EDI). EDI is a computer to computer exchange of documents between companies. EDI benefits include the elimination of redundant data entry and increased accuracy of downstream data.

If your company is interested in becoming an EDI trading partner with Florida Gas Transmission Co., see the FGT-Trading Partner Agreement , complete and forward to Jim Studebaker or Mary Draemer. If you have any additional EDI questions or comments concerning FGT's EDI program, give us a call.
The EDI Guides page contains information needed to implement EDI transactions with Florida Gas Transmission Co.. All of these documents are available in Microsoft Word format.
