Florida Gas Transmission Company (FGT) is a premier open access interstate pipeline company that transports natural gas for third parties, through its 4.7 thousand mile dual pipeline system which extends from Texas to Miami, Florida. FGT provides gas supply flexibility for its customers because of its proximity to various Gulf Coast producing regions and its interconnections with other interstate pipeline systems which provide access to virtually every other major natural gas producing region in the United States. FGT has a capacity of over 1.7 Bcf/d and currently transports natural gas to a portfolio of industrial customers, electric utilities and local distribution companies.

We are constantly expanding our pipeline to meet the clean energy needs of our customers.
